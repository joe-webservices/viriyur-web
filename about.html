About Viriyur

Discover the rich heritage and culture of our village

History

Viriyur has a rich historical background dating back centuries. The village has been a witness to various cultural and historical events that have shaped its identity.

Our ancestors have preserved many traditions and customs that continue to be an integral part of our daily lives.

Location

Located in the beautiful state of Tamil Nadu, Viriyur is surrounded by picturesque landscapes and natural beauty. The village is well-connected to major cities while maintaining its serene rural charm.

Culture

Our village is known for its vibrant culture, which includes:

  • Traditional festivals and celebrations
  • Local art forms and crafts
  • Traditional cuisine
  • Folk music and dance

Community

The people of Viriyur are known for their warmth and hospitality. We take pride in our strong community bonds and collective spirit.

Various community initiatives and programs help maintain the village's development while preserving its traditional values.

Important Landmarks

Ancient Temple

A historical temple that stands as a testament to our religious heritage

Village Lake

A serene water body that supports local agriculture and wildlife

Community School

An educational institution that has nurtured generations
